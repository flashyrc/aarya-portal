Who we are.

We are the story tellers. We love to hear an unheard story and try to convert it into the world’s best story ever told.

We tell the story in the forms of films. We believe that the films are the most effective way to communicate your story to the world. Most of the times the story of an organization or individual stays alive in the memories of different people. We bring all them together and make a beautiful collage by using unique styles and techniques of filmmaking.

How we do.

We Provide.

Organizational
Documentaries

Every organization has its own unique story. It’s a story of a common vision shared by many souls. It’s a story of becoming a grand tree from a tiny seed. It’s a story that is important to learn for the younger generation of that organization and also to inspire rest of the world.

Biographical
Films

Biographical films are the unique way to present the life and work of a great person whose vision and work influenced the life flow of countless individuals. These films inspire the future generation and help to impart the life values through a person’s life story.

CSR
Films

Most of the CSR Films focus on the capturing the social work and projects supported by the corporates through their CSR Initiative. But we see this whole endeavor from different perspective. We try to weave the story in such a manner that it unfolds the vision of a corporate entity for a better world.
